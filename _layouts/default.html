About this site

In the olden days of Cornell CS there was a wiki that ACSU maintained. Over the years it deteriorated, and eventually it was migrated to Wikia. Unfortunately, Wikia places ads on the wiki, and the wiki because used even less frequently. Fortunately, Kevin Chavez from OpenSourceCornell had saved the wiki as Markdown, and so the club members decided to migrate it to this site, which you are now reading.

This site is not sponsored by or endorsed by Cornell or the Computer Science department at Cornell. It is entirely student-run and student-written.
